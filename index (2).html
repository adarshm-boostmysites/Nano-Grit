Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does nano ceramic coating last?

+

Our professional-grade ceramic coatings last 2-7 years depending on the product and maintenance. We offer different packages based on your protection needs and budget. With proper care, some premium coatings can last even longer.

Is ceramic coating better than wax?

+

Yes, ceramic coating bonds chemically with your paint and lasts years, while wax only provides surface protection for a few months. Coating also offers superior protection against UV rays, chemicals, and scratches. It's a long-term investment in your vehicle's appearance.

How should I maintain my coated car?

+

We recommend regular washing with pH-neutral car shampoo, avoiding automatic car washes, and parking in covered areas when possible. We provide detailed maintenance guidelines after service. The hydrophobic properties make cleaning much easier than unprotected paint.

Can ceramic coating be applied to bikes?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in both cars and bikes, including complete restoration services with ceramic coating for motorcycles. We've successfully restored vintage bikes and applied protective coatings to modern motorcycles.
